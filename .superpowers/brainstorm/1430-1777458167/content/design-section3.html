Generation algorithm:
  • daysLeft = exam.exam_date − today (guard: min 1)
  • topics = filteredWeakTopics sorted by lowest accuracy
  • topicsPerDay = ceil(topics.length / daysLeft), max 3
  • action = daysLeft > 7 ? "Learn" : daysLeft > 3 ? "Practice" : "Revise"
  • Edge: 0 days → "Exam today — final revision checklist only"
  • Edge: no topics → "No weak areas — general revision schedule"
  • Edge: more days than topics → spread with rest/quiz days between

Section 3C — Integrations
