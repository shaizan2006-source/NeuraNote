The tradeoff

Option A: Hero Left

  • Asymmetric bento: Visual interest, premium feel
  • Ask AI dominates: Clear visual hierarchy — it's the flagship feature
  • Compact: 5 cards fit in one view, minimal scrolling
  • Desktop-optimized: Looks great on wide screens

Option B: Hero Top

  • Mobile-friendly: Scales better to narrow screens
  • Easier to scan: Linear top-to-bottom, no eye-jumping
  • Responsive: Can reflow to 2-col on tablet, 1-col on mobile
  • Symmetrical: Feels more orderly, less "designed"
