My take
Use glassmorphism (the middle option). It's elegant, it fits the design system (which already commits to glassmorphism for depth), and it gives the button a premium feel without glow or aggression. The glass effect says "this is premium" through translucency, not light.

The "liquid glass" variant (subtler) works if you want less frosted effect, but the standard glassmorphism is the sweet spot — it's noticeable and premium without feeling overdone.
